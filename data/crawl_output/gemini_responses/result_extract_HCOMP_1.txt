**About the Conference:**

The AAAI Conference on Human Computation and Crowdsourcing (HCOMP) is a premier venue for disseminating the latest research findings on human computation and crowdsourcing. It brings together researchers, engineers, and practitioners from various disciplines to discuss and exchange advances in the field. The conference covers a wide range of topics, including artificial intelligence, human-computer interaction, social computing, artificial intelligence economics, computational social science, digital humanities, policy, and ethics.
