 ECTEL 2025 
 The European Conference on Technology-Enhanced Learning (ECTEL) engages researchers, practitioners, educational developers, entrepreneurs, industry leaders and policy makers to address current challenges and advances in the field. ECTEL 2025 will take place on 15-19 September 2025 in Newcastle and Durham, UK, with the themeTwo Decades of TEL: from Lessons Learnt to Challenges Ahead. ECTEL 2025 will be a face-to-face conference.

 ECTEL 2025 
 We are excited to announce that the 20th European Conference on Technology Enhanced Learning (ECTEL) will take place in Newcastle and Durham, UK, in September 2025. As the flagship event of EATEL, ECTEL is one of the premier global conferences in the field of Technology Enhanced Learning. It brings together researchers, practitioners, educators, entrepreneurs, industry leaders, and policymakers to explore the latest challenges and innovations in TEL. Stay tuned for more details coming soon!
