  PhD Studentship  
 I am currently interested in recruiting new Ph.D. students in the fields related to Software Engineering, Quality Assurance, AI/Machine Learning, Software Automation, and their associated subjects. For more details regarding entry requirements and application procedure, please consult the doctoral study information page on the University of Auckland's official website.  
 If you are among the top students from reputable universities, there is the opportunity to apply for the | University of Auckland Doctoral Scholarship | , in addition to various | other postgraduate scholarships | and | country-specific scholarships | . 
  Chinese applicants | who are high-achieving students have the option to pursue the | China Scholarship Council (CSC) scholarship | , which is a collaboration between the University of Auckland and the | China Scholarship Council (CSC) | . 
  Furthermore, the New Zealand Government provides scholarships for citizens of eligible countries, such as the | international tertiary scholarships | .
