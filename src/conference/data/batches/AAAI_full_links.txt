 View AAAI-25 Photo Gallery    
   
  We are pleased to announce the Thirty-Ninth AAAI Conference on Artificial Intelligence (AAAI-25), which will be held in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania at the Pennsylvania Convention Center from February 25 to March 4, 2025.  
 The purpose of the AAAI conference series is to promote research in Artificial Intelligence (AI) and foster scientific exchange between researchers, practitioners, scientists, students, and engineers across the entirety of AI and its affiliated disciplines. AAAI-25 will feature technical paper presentations, special tracks, invited speakers, workshops, tutorials, poster sessions, senior member presentations, competitions, and exhibit programs, and a range of other activities to be announced.  
 Submission Site  
 AAAI-25 author registration and paper submissions will be accepted via OpenReview (https://openreview.net/).  
 OpenReview aims to promote openness in scientific communication, particularly the peer review process. Please review ‘Main Conference Timetable’ for related deadlines.

 What You’ll Learn  
 The AAAI-25 Conference will be held from February 27 to March 4, 2025, in Philadelphia. Organized by the Association for the Advancement of Artificial Intelligence (AAAI), this annual event brings together AI researchers , practitioners, and thought leaders to explore advancements in the field.  
 Attendees will engage in workshops, presentations, and discussions focused on cutting-edge research and applications in artificial intelligence. The event will provide opportunities to network with peers and explore the latest AI developments shaping various industries.  
 AAAI-25 Conference Themes  
 AI in Healthcare: | How AI can enhance diagnostics, patient care, and predictive analytics, relevant for healthcare executives, data scientists, and clinicians working on integrating AI into healthcare systems. 
  Ethics and AI: | Exploring | ethical challenges around AI | decision-making, particularly useful for policymakers, legal professionals, and technology leaders focused on responsible AI implementation. 
  Machine Learning and Deep Learning: | Advances in ML and DL technologies, critical for AI researchers, data engineers, and developers working on AI-driven products and services. 
  AI in Business Optimization: | AI’s role in optimizing business processes, valuable for business strategists, operations managers, and technology consultants aiming to enhance efficiency through AI. 
  AAAI-25 Personas  
 AI researchers developing new algorithms and models 
  Technology executives overseeing AI integration in their companies 
  Data scientists focused on advancing AI applications 
  Policy experts working on AI ethics and regulation 
  AAAI Current Product Offerings  
 Conferences and Workshops: | AAAI organizes annual conferences and workshops that address key AI advancements, providing research presentations and networking for professionals in AI development and applications. 
  Research Publications: | AAAI offers peer-reviewed journals, papers, and technical reports that focus on both theoretical and applied aspects of artificial intelligence. 
  AI Awards and Competitions: | AAAI runs award programs and challenges, recognizing innovations in AI and promoting competition among AI researchers and developers. 
  Educational Resources: | AAAI provides access to tutorials, seminars, and educational materials aimed at professionals and researchers looking to deepen their knowledge of AI technologies.
