Accepted papers will be published by MIT Press as open-access conference proceedings. Accepted submissions will be assigned an oral or poster presentation.\n*   **Summaries:** Limited to a maximum of 2 pages (not including references). Summaries must report on work that has been peer-reviewed and published already (e.g., in another conference or a journal). Summaries will not be included in the proceedings but will be made publicly available and will undergo a double-blind review process. Accepted submissions will be assigned an oral or poster presentation, but full papers will be given priority for oral presentations.\n*   **Late Breaking Abstracts:** Limited to a maximum of 2 pages (not including references).
