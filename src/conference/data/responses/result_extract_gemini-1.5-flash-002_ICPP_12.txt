  "Call for Papers": "# ICPP 2025: Call for Papers\n\nThe 54th International Conference on Parallel Processing (ICPP 2025) will be held at **The Catamaran Resort Hotel in San Diego, CA, from September 8-11, 2025.**\n\nThe conference theme is **“Looking Ahead in a Changing Landscape,”** highlighting the opportunities and changes taking place under the influence of AI and quantum computing.\n\n## Tracks\n\nICPP 2025 will be organized around eight tracks:\n\n1.  **System Architecture & Hardware Components:** Parallel Computer Architecture and Accelerator Designs, Large-Scale System Architectures, Datacenter/Warehouse Computing Architecture, Machine Learning Architectures, Micro-Architecture for Parallel Computing, Architectural Support for Networking, New Memory and Storage Technologies, Near-Memory Computing, Parallel I/O, Architectures for Edge Computing, Post-Moore, Architectural Support for Reliability and Security.\n2.  **Programming Environments & System Software:** System Software, Middleware, Runtimes for parallel computing, Parallel and Distributed Programming Languages & Models, Programming Systems, Compilers, Libraries, Programming Infrastructures and Tools, Operating and Real-Time Systems.\n3.  **Multidisciplinary:** Innovation combining multiple disciplines, Converged HPC Cloud Edge computing, Complex Workflows, Methodologies for Performance Portability and/or Productivity across Architectures.\n4.  **Algorithms:** Parallel and Distributed Algorithms, Parallel and Distributed Combinatorial & Numerical Methods, Scheduling Algorithms for Parallel and Distributed Applications and Platforms, Algorithmic Innovations for Parallel and Distributed Machine Learning, Post-Moore parallel algorithms.\n5.  **Performance:** Performance Modeling of Parallel or Distributed Computing, Performance Evaluation of Parallel or Distributed Systems; Scalability, Simulation Models, Analytical Models, Measurement-Based Evaluation.\n6.  **Applications & Use Cases:** Parallel, Distributed and Accelerated Applications, Scalable Data Analytics & Applied Machine Learning, Computational and Data-Driven Science & Engineering in computational sciences including, but not limited to Astrophysics, Computational Chemistry and Physics, Life Sciences, Earth Science, Materials Science, Finance, Geology and Engineering.\n7.  **AI in Computing:** AI for Application & Use Case, AI for System Architecture & Hardware Components, AI for Multidisciplinary, AI for Performance and AI for Programming Environments & Systems Software.\n8.  **Quantum Computing:** Parallel simulators of quantum computers, use of parallel computing for quantum compilation and optimization, co-design of parallel- and quantum-computing applications, hybrid parallel/quantum software-development tools.\n\n## Paper Submissions\n\n*   Paper length should be no more than 10 pages (including references) in the ACM SigConf format ([https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template](https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template)).\n*   The double-blind review process applies to all submissions.\n\n## Poster Submission\n\n*   Extended abstract length should be no more than 2 pages (including references) using ACM format ([https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template](https://www.acm.org/publications/proceedings-template)).\n\n## Submission Link\n\nAll submissions should be made at [https://ssl.linklings.net/conferences/icpp/](https://ssl.linklings.net/conferences/icpp/)\n\n## Important Dates\n\n*   **Workshop proposal submissions:** March 24, 2025\n*   **Workshop proposal notifications:** March 31, 2025\n*   **Paper Submission:** April 21, 2025\n*   **Author Notification:** June 10, 2025\n*   **Poster submission:** June 30, 2025\n*   **Poster Author notification:** July 15, 2025\n*   **Conference:** September 8-11, 2025"
