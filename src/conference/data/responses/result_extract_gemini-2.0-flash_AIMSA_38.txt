  "Call for Papers": "# AIMSA 2024: Call for Papers\n\nThe 19th International Conference on Artificial Intelligence: Methodology, Systems, Applications (AIMSA 2024) will be held in **Golden Sands resort, Varna, Bulgaria, from September 18th-20th, 2024**.\n\n## Description\n\nAIMSA is a premier single-track forum for exchanging information and research results on theory and principles of artificial intelligence (AI) along with real-world applications of intelligent systems technology.\n\nThe conference brings together researchers and practitioners in all areas of AI from both academia and industry to meet and share their ideas and experiences and learn about the recent trends and developments in contemporary AI.\n\n## Topics\n\nThe conference is looking for submissions offering innovative methods, systems, and applications that demonstrate efficiency and sustainability for the future of AI. Efficiency can be demonstrated against current and traditional methods, systems, and applications. Sustainability relates to the required resources, computational power, human wellbeing, positive impact on science, and society.\n\nThe conference will have three streams:\n\n*   Innovative methods for efficient and sustainable AI\n*   Systems for the development and the implementations of efficient and sustainable AI\n*   Novel efficient and sustainable AI applications\n\n## Submission and Review Process\n\nAll submissions will be subject to a single-blind academic peer review by at least three members of the program committee. Selection criteria include accuracy and originality of ideas, clarity and significance of results, and quality of presentation.\n\nThe author(s) of the best paper (which will be selected based on the votes of the attendees) of the conference will receive the Best Paper Award during the conference.\n\n## Important Dates\n\n*   **Deadline for acceptance is until:** August 20, 2024\n*   **Deadline for registration (full papers):** August 25, 2024\n*   **Deadline for registration (abstracts):** August 31, 2024\n*   **Conference:** September 18-20, 2024\n\n## Programme Committee Chair\n\nNikola Kasabov\nAuckland University of Technology, Auckland, New Zealand\n\n## Local Organizing Committee Chair\n\nPetia Koprinkova-Hristova\nInstitute of Information and Communication Technologies\nBulgarian Academy of Sciences\n\n## Proceedings\n\nThe AIMSA 2024 Proceedings will be published by Springer Nature in their Lecture Notes in Artificial Intelligence subline of the Lecture Notes in Computer Science series.\n\n## Language\n\nThe official language of the conference is English."
