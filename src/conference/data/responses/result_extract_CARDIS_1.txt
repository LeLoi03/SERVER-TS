## CASCADE 2025 Conference Information:

**Date:** April 2 - 4, 2025
**Location:** Telecom Saint-Etienne, Saint-Etienne, France
**Topic:** Cryptographic implementations, physical attacks, embedded security

**Important Dates:**

**Summer Submission**

* Paper submission: October 1, 2024
* Result notification: November 29, 2024

**Winter Submission**

* Paper submission: December 17, 2024
* Result notification: February 22, 2025

**Registration**

* Early registration deadline: February 28, 2025

**Other Information:**

* CASCADE is a new conference resulting from the merger of COSADE and CARDIS.
* The conference will focus on cryptographic implementations, physical attacks, and embedded security challenges.
* Contributions are welcome from academia and industry, especially from European PhD students.
* Submissions should follow Springer's LNCS format and must be written in English.
* Submissions must be original, unpublished, anonymous, and not submitted to other journals or conferences.
* The proceedings will be published in the Springer Lecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS) series.
