 2025 USENIX Annual Technical Conference 
 July 7–9, 2025 
 Boston, MA, USA 
 Co-located with 
 OSDI '25 
 The2025 USENIX Annual Technical Conferencewill take place on July 7–9, 2025, at the Sheraton Boston in Boston, MA, USA. 
 USENIX ATC brings together leading systems researchers for the presentation of cutting-edge systems research and the opportunity to gain insight into a wealth of must-know topics, including virtualization, system and network management and troubleshooting, cloud and edge computing, security, privacy, and trust, mobile and wireless, and more.

 2024 USENIX Annual Technical Conference 
 July 10–12, 2024 
 Santa Clara, CA, USA 
 Co-located with 
 OSDI '24 
 Thanks to those who joined us for the 2024 USENIX Annual Technical Conference (USENIX ATC '24). We hope you enjoyed the event. 
 USENIX ATC brings together leading systems researchers for the presentation of cutting-edge systems research and the opportunity to gain insight into a wealth of must-know topics, including virtualization, system and network management and troubleshooting, cloud and edge computing, security, privacy, and trust, mobile and wireless, and more. 
 As part of our commitment to open access to research, the full proceedings and presentation slides are free and open to the public on thehref="https://www.usenix.org/conference/atc24/technical-sessions" - technical sessions page
. Videos are posted within a few weeks of the end of the event. 
 Registration Information 
 Early Bird Registration Deadline: Monday, June 17, 2024 
 See thehref="https://www.usenix.org/conference/atc24/registration-information" - Registration Information page
for details, including fees, discount information, and other deadlines. 
 Discounts 
 In addition to ourhref="https://www.usenix.org/conference/atc24/registration-information#discounts" - member discounts
, USENIX offershref="https://www.usenix.org/conference/atc24/registration-discounts" - several discounts
to help you to attend USENIX ATC '24, including a hardship discount for USENIX members who are unemployed or retired, in between jobs, or lack employer support. 
 USENIX is committed to ensuring that our meetings are fully accessible to all attendees. Visit ouraccessibility information pagefor details on mothers' rooms, ADA accessibility accommodation options, and how to make a request. 
 Venue 
 Hyatt Regency Santa Clara 
 5101 Great America Parkway 
 Santa Clara, CA, 95054 
 USA 
 +1 408.200.1234 
 Hotel Reservation Deadline:Monday, June 17, 2024Monday, July 1, 2024 (Deadline Extended!) 
 USENIX has negotiated a special conference attendee room rate of US$289 for single/double occupancy for conference attendees, including in-room wireless internet. To receive this rate,book your room onlineor call the hotel directly and mention USENIX or ATC '24 when making your reservation. 
 The group rate will be available until Monday, July 1, or until the block sells out, whichever occurs first. After this date, contact the hotel directly to inquire about room availability. 
 Room and Ride Sharing 
 USENIX maintains ahref="https://groups.google.com/a/usenix.org/d/forum/roomshare" - Google Group
to facilitate communication among attendees seeking roommates and ride sharing. You can sign up for free to find attendees with whom you can share a hotel room, taxi, shuttle, or other ride-share service. Please include"USENIX ATC '24"in the subject line when posting a new request.
