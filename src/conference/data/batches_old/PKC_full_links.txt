 Public Key Cryptography (PKC) 
 The International Conference on Practice and Theory in Public Key Cryptography (PKC) been sponsored by the IACR since 2003. PKC has been the main annual conference focusing on all aspects of public-key cryptography, attracting cutting-edge results from world-renowned scientists in the area. PKC oversight is provided by thePKC Steering Committee 
 PKC now has aTest of Time Award.
