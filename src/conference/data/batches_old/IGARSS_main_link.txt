 Welcome to Brisbane, 
 IGARSS 2025! 
 The International Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium (IGARSS) is the flagship conference of the IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (GRSS). On behalf of the Organizing Committee, we are thrilled to invite you to attend the 45th IGARSS which is to be held in the sunshine city Brisbane Australia, on 3-8 August 2025. 
 The theme ofIGARSS 2025 is One Earth. IGARSS 2025 will address the threats to our Earth and promote collaborative global solutions using remote sensing technology. The event will provide an excellent experience for IGARSS attendees through strong technical and social programs, and opportunities for collaboration regionally and globally. 
 Australia is a safe multicultural country and August is a great time to visit Brisbane and the state of Queensland more broadly. The conference will be hosted at the Brisbane Convention & Exhibition Centre (BCEC), which is a world-class conference venue.

 On behalf of the IEEE Geoscience and Remote Sensing Society (GRSS) and the IGARSS 2025 Organising Committee, we are pleased to invite you to submit your research papers to the 45th IEEE International Geoscience and Remote Sensing Symposium, which will take place on 3-8 August 2025, in the beautiful sunshine city of Brisbane, Australia. As the flagship conference of the IEEE GRSS, IGARSS 2025 is set to be an exceptional event, bringing together the global community of geoscience and remote sensing professionals. 
 Conference Theme: "One Earth" 
 The IGARSS 2025 theme is 'One Earth' to highlight the essential contribution of remote sensing in tackling global environmental issues. The conference aims to foster international cooperation in developing global solutions to safeguard our planet, positioning it as an essential gathering for researchers dedicated to advancing remote sensing technologies for a sustainable future. 
 Topics of Interest: 
 We encourage submissions that cover a wide range ofhref="topics.php" - topics
, including but not limited to: 
 Imaging and Processing Techniques 
 Remote Sensing Data Analysis 
 AI and Big Data. 
 Electromagnetic Emission and Scattering Modeling 
 Remote Sensing Applications to Atmosphere, Cryosphere, Land, or Oceans 
 Remote Sensing of Planetary and other Celestial Bodies 
 Mission, Platforms and Calibration 
 Sensors, Instruments and Calibration 
 Education and Policy 
 Societal Engagement and Impacts 
 Submission Guidelines: 
 Authors are invited tosubmit abstracts or papers in one of the three options belowthrough thehref="papers.php" - IGARSS 2025 online submission portal
by10 January 202517 January 2025.Note that the paper submission with these three options is NEW and different from previous IGARSS.Submission options are: 
 Full paper: 4-page limit, excluding references; required for inclusion in IEEE Xplore or consideration for the Student Paper Competition 
 Abstract: 400-600 words, will not be published in IEEE Xplore 
 Recently Published Article: Papers published as peer-reviewed articles after January 1, 2024 in relevant journals ranked within | Q1 based on the current International Scientific Indexing (ISI) or Scientific Journal Ranking (SJR) | . 
 All submissions will undergo a peer-review process. Only the accepted full papers (option 1 above) will be included in the conference proceedings and published in IEEE Xplore, ensuring high visibility and impact.
