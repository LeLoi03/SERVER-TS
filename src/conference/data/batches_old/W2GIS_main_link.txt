 22ndInternational Symposium 
 Web and Wireless Geographical Information Systems 
 September8-9, 2025 -Mexico City,México 
 W2GIS 2025 
 W2GIS 2025 
 Recent advances in wireless Internet and location-based services have generated increasing interest in the diffusion and processing of large volumes of geospatial data. Then, new challenges and opportunities have appeared in the GISscience research community. This symposium provides an up-to-date review of advances in both theoretical and technical development of Web and Wireless Geographical Information Systems (W2GIS). It is the 22nd in a series of events alternating locations between East Asia, Europe and America. 
 Call for Papers 
 W2GIS 2025invites high-qualitysubmissionsof three types of contributions:full papers,work in progress papers,andshowcase papers. 
 More details are available on thehref="/view/w2gis-2025/call-for-papers" - Call for Papers
page. 
 Doctoral Symposium 
 W2GIS 2025 will also feature aDoctoral Symposium, providinga valuable platform forPh.D studentsto receive feedback and guidance from well-known researchers and industry experts. 
 More details are available on thehref="/view/w2gis-2025/doctoral-symposium" - Doctoral Symposium
page. 
 Important Dates 
 Submission deadline:8 March 202523 March 2025 
 Thank you for your interest and support, we are now in the peer review phase! 
 Notification of acceptance:20May2025 
 Camera-Ready copy due:5June2025 
  
 Author registration deadline:25July2025 
 Early-bird registration:25 May 2025 
  
 Awards 
 At the conference, two500 € prizeswill be awarded for thebest paperand thebest presentation. 
  
 Grants 
 W2GIS 2025 offerstwo grantsfor Ph.D and master students, covering the conference registration fees. 
 Apply Here. 
 Dissemination 
 All the accepted papers will be published in the conference proceedings in the SpringerLecture Notes in Computer Science (LNCS)series, which is indexed on Clarivate Analytics’ Web of Science, Scopus, EI Engineering Index, Google Scholar, DBLP and other platforms. 
 The proceedings ofpreviousconferences are availableonlineand can be accessed by clicking on the image below. 

The authors of selected papers will be invited to submit an extended version of their work for consideration in a dedicatedSpecial Issueof theCuadernos de Geografía: Revista Colombiana de Geografíajournal (Q4on Scopus).
