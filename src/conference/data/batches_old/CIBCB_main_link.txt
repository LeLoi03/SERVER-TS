 2025 IEEE Conference on Computational Intelligence in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology 
 – AI Drives Biomedical, Bioinformatics and Healthcare – 
 National Cheng Kung University Department of Electrical Engineering is located at the Ziqiang Campus of National Cheng Kung University. It has the largest building of the Department of Electrical Engineering in Taiwan called Chimei Building. It is a twelve-story building. It has a spacious environment for students to study and presentation, including the lecture hall UMC Rm and the classroom L. Y. Rm. 
 The conference will be held at the Building of Electrical Engineering, located on the National Cheng-Kung University (NCKU) Tzu-Chiang Campus, located just a 10-minute walk from the rear entrance to the Tainan Station.
