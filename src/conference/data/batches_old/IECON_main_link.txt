Welcome 
 IEEE IECON is the flagship yearly conference of the IEEE Industrial Electronics Society, devoted to the dissemination of new ideas, research and works in progress in the fields of Robotics and Mechatronics, Power Electronics, Cybersecurity, Renewable Energy, Smart Grid, Digital Twins, AI for Industrial Processes, and Industry 5.0, among others. 
 IECON program includes workshops, tutorials, panels, keynotes, and presentation of original research and industrial applications. 
 The world’s industry, research, and academia are cordially invited to participate in a wide array of technical and social activities for the advancement of science, technology, industry, engineering education, and fellowship.

 How to get to Madrid 
 Madrid has just one airportserving all the routes arriving to and departing from the city. Adolfo Suarez Madrid-Barajas Airport is located in the northeast of Madrid, Barajas district, only 8 miles (13 kilometers) from Madrid city center. 
 It has four terminals and one satellite building,as Terminal (T4) consists of two buildings, T4 and its satellite (T4S), connected through an automatic underground train. The shuttle bus connecting the terminals is free and operates 24/7. 
 Medical services, ATMs, VAT refunds, and police assistance are among the services offered at the airport, along with a multitude of shops and restaurants. 
 Madrid is a friendly and beautiful city is the most important city in Spain. The city has been able to successfully mix traditional culture and fine gastronomy with a new vision for the future and respectfully renders tribute to its past while hosting landmark buildings of vanguardism architecture. 
 We look forward to an exciting time in Madrid, full of science, applications in industrial electronics, culture and gastronomy!

Prospective authors are invited to submit their manuscript using the template and submission above, adhering to the following limitations: 
 • Up to 6 full pages, including paper title, authors and affiliations, figures and references (maximum size 2MB). Up to 2 additional pages are allowed for a fee. 
 • Only PDF files are allowed 
 For accepted papers to be included in the conference proceedings and submitted for possible inclusion in the IEEE Xplore® Digital Library, the following requirements must be satisfied by the corresponding deadline: 
 • Submission of the final version of the paper 
 • Transfer copyright of the paper to IEEE 
 • At least one of the authors must pay full registration for the conference 
 • The paper must be presented at the conference
