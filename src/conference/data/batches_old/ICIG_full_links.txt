 Conference Overview 
 The 11th International Conference on Image and Graphics (ICIG) will be held in Haikou, China, on December 26 – 28, 2021. The Conference will be sponsored by China Society of Image and Graphics, organized by Hainan University. ICIG is a biennial conference that focuses on innovative technologies of image, video and graphics processing and fostering innovation, entrepreneurship, and networking. It will feature world-class plenary speakers, exhibits, and high quality peer reviewed oral and poster presentations.
