 ISCA 2025 will be held at Waseda University, Shinjuku, Tokyo! 
  
 TheInternational Symposium on Computer Architecture(ISCA) is the premier forum for new ideas and experimental results in computer architecture. The conference seeks particularly forward-looking and novel submissions. In 2025, the 52ndedition of ISCA will be held in Tokyo, Japan at Waseda University during June 21 – 25, 2025. 
  
 See you all in Tokyo!
