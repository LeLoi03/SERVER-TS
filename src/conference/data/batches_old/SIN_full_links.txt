 2024 17th International Conference on Security of Information and Networks (SIN) 
 href="https://www.sinconf.org/sin2024/" - 2024 17th International Conference on Security of Information and Networks (SIN)
took place December 2-4, 2024 in Sydney, New South Wales, Australia. 
 The 17th International Conference on Security of Information and Networks (SIN'24) was technically sponsored by IEEE and the IEEE NSW Section. SIN'24 provided an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge and results in theory, methodology, and applications of Security in Information and Networks. SIN'24 included keynote presentations, refereed technical papers and workshops addressing all aspects and issues of Security in Information and Networks. 
 SIN'24 also incorporated The 1st Workshop on Artificial Intelligence for Networked Drone and Sensor Applications (DroneSense-AI 2024).

The International Conference on Information Systems Security and Privacy is an event where researchers and practitioners can meet and discuss state-of-the-art research about the technological, social, and regulatory challenges that regard the security, privacy, and trust of modern information systems. The conference welcomes papers of either practical or theoretical nature, and is interested in research or applications addressing all aspects of trust, security and privacy, and encompassing issues of concern for organizations, individuals and society at large.
