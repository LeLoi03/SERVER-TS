 IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV 2025) 
 28 February - 4 March, 2025 in Tucson, Arizona 
 IEEE Computer Society is excited to announce the upcominghref="https://wacv2025.thecvf.com/" - IEEE/CVF Winter Conference on Applications of Computer Vision (WACV 2025)
an event focused on the field of computer vision, featuring a main conference along with several co-located workshops and tutorials. With its high quality and low cost, it provides an exceptional value for students, academics and industry researchers. 
 At WACV 2025, you'll explore cutting-edge advancements in computer vision, engage with groundbreaking research, and connect with leading experts from around the globe. This conference offers a unique opportunity to learn, exchange ideas, and expand your professional network within the vibrant computer vision community.
