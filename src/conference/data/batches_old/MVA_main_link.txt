 Call for Papers 
 19th International Conference on Machine Vision Applications 
 July 26–28, 2025 
 Kyoto International Conference Center, Kyoto, Japan 
 The Nineteenth International Conference on Machine Vision Applications will be held in Kyoto, Japan, from July 26 through 28, 2025. The conference is sponsored by IEICE-ISS. This conference aims to bring together researchers and practitioners from both academia and industry and to stimulate the exchange of knowledge through intensive discussions on cutting-edge research topics on machine vision and its applications.

 Submission 
 The page limit is 4 pages, excluding references. 
 The proceedings of MVA2025 will be published in | IEICE Proceeding Series | and submitted for inclusion into | IEEE Xplore | subject to meeting IEEE Xplore’s scope and quality requirements. 
 Important Notes 
 At least one of the authors for each submission must serve as a reviewer. | This reviewer must have a Ph.D. or be a Ph.D. student and have experience with peer review. If no such reviewer is available, it is not necessary to provide a reviewer to that submission. Additionally, the same person can serve as a reviewer for multiple submissions. 
 You may not submit papers that are under review at other conferences or journals. However,if you wish, you can simultaneously submit the same paper tothe special issue [DV ISS Symposium (MVA2025)] inIEICE Transactions on Information and Systems. | In this case, the paper will be reviewed independently from MVA, and if accepted, it will be published as a letter in the IEICE Transactions. Please note that the acceptance results for MVA and IEICE Transactions may not necessarily match. | If the paper is accepted by both, it will not be published in the MVA proceedings. | Additionally, the publication fee will be the responsibility of each author. If you wish to submit simultaneously to the IEICE Transactions on Information and Systems, please visit the following submission site and select [DV ISS Symposium (MVA2025) and Transactions Concurrent Review System] at | href="https://review.ieice.org/regist/regist_baseinfo_e.aspx" - the submission site | . | href="https://mva-org.jp/mva2025/data/uploads/guide-for-submission-under-the-iss-symposium-and-transactions-concurrent-review-system.pdf" - [PDF] Guide for Submission under the ISS Symposium and Transactions Concurrent Review System
