 About MICC 2025 
 The 2025 IEEE 17th Malaysia International Conference on Communications (MICC) is fully sponsored byIEEE Malaysia Communication Society & Vehicular Technology Society Joint Chapter(ComSoc / VTS) will be held on the 27 – 28 August 2025. The conference will be held at Malacca, Malaysia. MICC has since been organized biennially and has been a few times organized in collaboration with other major conferences. In 1997, MICC was held in conjunction with IEEE 5th International Workshop on Intelligent Signal Processing and Communications Systems (ISPACS’97). In 2003 it was co-organized with IEEE 9th Asia-Pacific Conference on Communication (APCC03) and in 2005, the 7th MICC was organized in conjunction with IEEE 13th International Conference on Networks (ICON05). The 8th MICC in 2007 was co-hosted with the 14th International Conference on Telecommunications (ITC07). Next, the 9th MICC in 2009 was held in Kuala Lumpur. The latest, the 10th MICC in 2011 was held in Kota Kinabalu that was collocated with the 17th Asia-Pacific Conference on Communications (APCC 2011). The 11th MICC2013 was held at Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, and we have celebrated 20th year celebration of this conference. MICC conferences have been very well attended and supported by researchers from Asia-Pacific region and the world at large. The 12th MICC2015 and 13thMICC2017 was successfully held in Kuching, Sarawak and Johor Bahru, Johor and the 14th MICC2019 was held in Hotel Bangi-Putrajaya, Selangor. The 15th MICC2021 was held virtually due to Covid-19 pandemic. While, the 16th MICC2023 was held in Kuala Lumpur with the theme “Smart Digital Communication for Humanity”. Finally, the 17th MICC2025 will be expected to be held in Grand Swiss-Belhotel Melaka. 
 Accepted papers will be published in theMICC2025 Conference Proceedingand will be submitted to IEEE Xplore for their consideration to be published. The proceedings also will be submitted for indexing and available at major academic databases such asSCOPUS. The proceeding of the previous MICC is available inIEEE XploreandSCOPUSdatabases.  Malaysian International Cocoa Fair 2025 
 Posted at hinFestivals,Internationalbyhref="https://chocolateaffairsmagazine.com/author/cameditor/" - Chocolate Affairs Magazine
May 24 – 27, 2025 
 Sabah International Convention Centre (SICC), Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia 
 https://micf.koko.gov.my 
 Thehref="https://micf.koko.gov.my/" - Malaysian International Cocoa Fair 2025 (MICF 2025)
is poised to be a landmark event in the global cocoa industry, bringing together stakeholders from around the world to engage, innovate, and celebrate the rich heritage of cocoa. Scheduled from May 24 to 27, 2025, at the Sabah International Convention Centre (SICC) in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia, this event promises a comprehensive exploration of the cocoa sector’s latest advancements and opportunities. 
 Event Highlights 
 Malaysian International Cocoa Conference 2025 (MICC 2025) 
 Taking place on May 26 and 27, this conference aims to convene approximately 1,000 local and international delegates, including producers, researchers, policymakers, and industry experts. Themedhref="https://micf.koko.gov.my/micc/" - “Embracing Cocoa Challenges for Sustainable Excellence,” MICC 2025
will feature: 
 Opening Ceremony: | Inaugurated by YB Datuk Seri Johari bin Abdul Ghani, Minister of Plantation and Commodities. 
 World Cocoa Scenario Session: | Presented by Mr. Michel Arrion, Executive Director of the International Cocoa Organization (ICCO). 
 Plenary Lectures and Expert Panels: | Discussions on topics such as cocoa traceability, price trajectories, and the future of the cocoa industry in Asia. 
 Networking Opportunities: | Including VVIP tours, exhibition visits, and networking lunches to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among attendees. 
 Concurrent Sessions: | Covering a range of topics from high-yield opportunities in cocoa plantations to innovations in cocoa and chocolate production. 
 These sessions are designed to address pressing challenges and opportunities within the cocoa industry, fostering meaningful collaboration and knowledge sharing among global cocoa stakeholders. 
 Malaysian International Cocoa Exhibition 2025 (MICE 2025) 
 Running throughout the event from May 24 to 27, thisexhibitionwill showcase the latest innovations, technologies, and services in the cocoa industry. Key features include: 
 International Cocoa Business Matching (ICBM): | Facilitating global partnerships and collaborations. 
 Cocoa Business Matching (CBM): | Providing a platform for local businesses to connect and explore opportunities. 
 Business Opportunity & Sharing Sessions: | Offering insights into market trends and business strategies. 
 Handmade Chocolate Competition & Workshop: | Celebrating artisanal skills and encouraging knowledge transfer. 
 Chocolate Sculpture Competition: | Showcasing creativity and craftsmanship in chocolate artistry. 
 Bean to Bar Chocolate Demonstration: | Educating attendees on the complete chocolate-making process. 
 TikTok Live Streaming & Chocolate Challenge: | Engaging the digital audience with interactive content. 
 Cocoa Lucky Draw: | Adding an element of excitement and rewarding participation. 
 href="https://chocolateaffairsmagazine.com/big-island-chocolate-festival-a-sweet-cirque-extravaganza/" - See also  Big Island Chocolate Festival 2025
These activities aim to empower the cocoa industry by providing a dynamic platform for collaboration, innovation, knowledge exchange, promotion of cocoa products and technology, and celebration of industry achievements. 
 Malaysian International Cocoa Dinner 2025 (MICD 2025) 
 Scheduled for the evening of May 26, thisgala dinnerwill feature cultural and celebrity performances, along with the MICF 2025 Appreciation Awards, recognizing outstanding achievements in the global cocoa industry. 
 Malaysian International Cocoa Fair Venue and Date 
 MICF 2025 will be hosted at the Sabah International Convention Centre (SICC) in Kota Kinabalu, Sabah, Malaysia, from May 24 to 27, 2025. Sabah, known as the ‘Land Below The Wind,’ is a region rich in cultural heritage and natural beauty, offering attendees an opportunity to explore Malaysia as a premium cocoa and chocolate destination. 
 Why Attend Malaysian International Cocoa Fair (MICF) 2025? 
 MICF 2025 is designed to provide numerous benefits to attendees, including: 
 Business Growth: | Opportunities to expand networks and explore new markets. 
 Educational Value: | Access to the latest research, market trends, and sustainable practices within the cocoa sector. 
 Sustainability & Social Impact: | Discussions on sustainable practices and social responsibility in cocoa production. 
 Regulatory Updates: | Insights into the latest regulations affecting the cocoa industry. 
 Networking Opportunities: | Engagement with industry leaders, experts, and peers. 
 Innovation: | Exposure to breakthroughs in cocoa technology and production methods. 
 The event also aims to boost the local economy, promote tourism, and highlight Malaysia’s culture and heritage, reinforcing the country’s position as a regional cocoa hub. 
 A Note From Our Editor 
 TheMalaysian International Cocoa Fair 2025is a must-attend event for anyone passionate about the future of cocoa and chocolate. As the industry evolves with new sustainability challenges, technological innovations, and shifting consumer demands, MICF 2025 offers a unique platform to connect with global experts, cocoa producers, chocolatiers, and industry leaders. 
 From insightful discussions at theMalaysian International Cocoa Conferenceto hands-on experiences at the Cocoa Exhibition, this event provides an invaluable opportunity to discover the latest trends, explore new business prospects, and celebrate the artistry of chocolate-making. Whether you’re a seasoned professional, an aspiring chocolatier, or simply a cocoa enthusiast, MICF 2025 is where the industry’s future takes shape.
