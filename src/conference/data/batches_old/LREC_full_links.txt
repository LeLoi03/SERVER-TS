 LREC 
 Language Resources and Evaluation Conference 
 TheLREC Conferencehas become a major event in the field of language engineering, and constitutes a milestone in the life of Human Language Technologies (HLT). It is organised by ELRA biennially with the support of institutions and organisations involved in HLT, and brings together a large number of people working and interested in HLT.
