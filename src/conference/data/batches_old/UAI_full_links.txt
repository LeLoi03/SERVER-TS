Call for Papers 
 The Conference on Uncertainty in Artificial Intelligence (href="https://auai.org/" - UAI
) is one of the premier international conferences on research related to learning and reasoning in the presence of uncertainty. The conference has been held every year since 1985. The upcoming 41st edition (UAI 2025) will be an in-person conference with virtual elements taking place in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil from July 21st to July 25th 2025. 
 We invite papers that describe novel theory, methodology and applications related to artificial intelligence, machine learning and statistics. Papers will be assessed in a rigorous double-blind peer-review process, based on the criteria of technical correctness, novelty, whether claims are backed up convincingly, and clarity of writing. Authors are strongly encouraged to make code and data available. 
 All accepted papers will be presented in poster sessions and spotlight presentations (physically or remotely). Selected papers will have longer presentations. All accepted papers will be published in a volume ofhref="http://proceedings.mlr.press/" - Proceedings of Machine Learning Research (PMLR)
. 
 Important dates for authors: 
 January 5th, 2025 (23:59 AoE): Paper submission starts 
 Suggested OpenReview account creation deadline: January 27, 2025. (If you do not already have an OpenReview account, please register by this date, otherwise we cannot guarantee that your account will be activated in time.) 
 February 10th, 2025 (23:59 AoE): Paper submission deadline 
 April 3rd: Reviews release 
 April 3rd-12th, 2025: Discussion period 
 May 6th, 2025 (23:59 AoE): Author notification 
 Papers should be submitted on thehref="https://openreview.net/group?id=auai.org/UAI/2025/Conference" - UAI 2025 OpenReview submission site
. Please see thehref="https://www.auai.org/uai2025/submission_instructions" - Submission Instructions
for more details on how your manuscript should be formatted. 
 Note thatwe require having an OpenReview account to submit. It is strongly recommended that you sign-up for OpenReviewwith an institutional email address, so it can be activated immediately. If your institution email address is not recognized, please contact OpenReview support via their Feedback form. 
 We are looking forward to building an exciting program and we aim to make the most of the advantages that a hybrid conference can create. 
 Relevant dates: 
 July 21st, 2025: Tutorials 
 July 22nd-24th, 2025: Main conference 
 July 25th, 2025: Workshops
