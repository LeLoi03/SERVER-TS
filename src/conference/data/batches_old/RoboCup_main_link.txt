 RoboCup 2025 arrives in the vibrant city ofSalvador, one of Brazil's most iconic destinations, bringing theworld’s largest Robotics and Artificial Intelligence event. 
 An event with many challengesIn its 28th edition, the event now covers all the major scientific and technological challenges of autonomous robotics. 
 A competition, but not only...It brings together thousands of researchers from all over the world around a common ambition: to share knowledge and innovation. 
 FromJuly 15th to 21st, researchers, students, enthusiasts, and companies from around the globe will gather to compete, learn, and inspire new technological frontiers. The event, renowned for its innovative competitions, challenges teams todevelop autonomous robotscapable of operating in real-world scenarios, such assoccer matches,rescue operations, andservice robotics.
