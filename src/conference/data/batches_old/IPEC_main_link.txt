 September 15-19, 2025, Warsaw, Poland 
 General information 
 ALGO is an annual meeting combining the premier algorithmic conferencehref="https://algo-conference.org/2025/esa/" - European Symposium on Algorithms (ESA)
and a number of other specialized conferences and workshops, all related to algorithms and their applications, making ALGO the major European event for researchers, students and practitioners in algorithms. 
 ALGO 2025 will be held at theFaculty of Physics at the University of Warsawin Warsaw, Poland. 
 This website is still in preparation. More information as well as the subpages of the conferences will become available at a later date.
