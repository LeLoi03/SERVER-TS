 The University of Catania is proud to host the 15th edition of theInternational Conference on Formal Ontology in Information Systems(FOIS) and theJoint Ontology Workshops Episode XI(JOWO) 2025, the flagship conference and joint workshops of theInternational Association of Ontology and its Applications (IAOA).

 FOIS Online: September 4-5, 2025. 
 JOWO 2025 and FOIS 2025: September 8-12, 2025.
