 The Web Applications Society (@WAS) is a non-profit international organization dedicated to advancing information integration and web Intelligence. It emerged from the success of the iiWAS series of workshops and conferences, which highlighted the need for a formal body to continue organizing such events and promote collaboration in this field. 
  
 @WAS focuses on fostering research, development, and dissemination of knowledge related to web applications and services. It serves as a platform for professionals, researchers, and enthusiasts to share insights, discuss challenges, and explore innovative solutions in the rapidly evolving domain of web technologies. 
  
 The organization is known for hosting conferences, workshops, and seminars that bring together experts from academia and industry. These events provide opportunities for networking, collaboration, and staying updated with the latest trends and advancements in web applications. 
 The scope of @WAS actions is threefold:steering the organization of the iiWAS, MoMM and DEXA conferences and workshops,editing or endorsing scientific publications (books, journals, special issues of journals, and proceedings of meetings), andorganizing or endorsing events (workshop, seminars), and projects.
