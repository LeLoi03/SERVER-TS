 The Innovations in Intelligent Systems and Applications (ASYU) Conference, which has been organized since 2004, will be hosted this year by Bursa Technical University on September 10-12, 2025. The conference languages are Turkish and English, and special session proposals on current topics related to smart applications in fields such as healthcare, energy, transportation, engineering education, defense industry, finance, business, and similar areas will be accepted. All papers submitted to the conference will be evaluated through a double-blind peer review process by at least three reviewers.The conference will be held in person (physical). For international participants only, an online participation option will also be available.Conference Topics 
 We invite submissions of original, unpublished technical papers on topics including but not limited to:
