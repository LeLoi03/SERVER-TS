 About ISWC 2025 
 The International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) is the premier international forum for the Semantic Web / Linked Data Community. ISWC2025 will bring together researchers, practitioners, and industry specialists to discuss, advance, and shape the future of semantic technologies. ISWC offers five exciting and fruitful days you don’t want to miss every year! 
  
 ISWC2025 will be an in-person conference. We look forward to meeting you in Nara, Japan, in November 2025. 

To make the conference more environmentally friendly the Semantic Web Association (SWSA) is offsetting the carbon footprint of ISWC 2025.  International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) 
 The International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) is the leading conference for research on Semantic Web topics. It is held annually and is the successor of the Semantic Web Working Symposium (SWWS). 
 TheSWSA Ten-Year Awardrecognizes most influential papers from the ISWC proceedings ten years prior. 
 Thehref="http://swsa.semanticweb.org/content/code-conduct-iswc" - Code of conduct for ISWC
delineates the behavioural norms expected of ISWC attendees. 
 TheSemantic Web Conference Corpusprovides access to information about all ISWC conferences in RDF. You can browse and search information on papers that were presented, people who attended, and other things that have to do with the main conferences and workshops.

 About ISWC 2025 
 The International Semantic Web Conference (ISWC) is the premier international forum for the Semantic Web / Linked Data Community. ISWC2025 will bring together researchers, practitioners, and industry specialists to discuss, advance, and shape the future of semantic technologies. ISWC offers five exciting and fruitful days you don’t want to miss every year! 
  
 ISWC2025 will be an in-person conference. We look forward to meeting you in Nara, Japan, in November 2025. 

To make the conference more environmentally friendly the Semantic Web Association (SWSA) is offsetting the carbon footprint of ISWC 2025.
