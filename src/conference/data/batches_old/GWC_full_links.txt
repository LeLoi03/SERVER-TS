 Global WordNet Grid 
 The building of a completely free worldwideGlobal WordNet Grid, which will be build around a shared set of concepts used in many wordnet projects. 
 We invite people from all language communities to upload synsets from their language to the Grid. Gradually, the Grid will then be represented by all languages.
